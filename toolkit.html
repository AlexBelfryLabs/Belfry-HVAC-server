The 8 Biggest Money Leaks

01 -- After-Hours Lead Loss

40-60% of HVAC calls come outside business hours. Without auto-response, those leads call the next company. At $450/job, 5 missed leads/week = $9,000+/month gone.

02 -- No-Show Appointments

Industry no-show rate is 15-25% without reminders. A $300k/yr company loses $45-75k in wasted truck rolls. Automated SMS reminders cut this by 70%+.

03 -- Slow Estimate Follow-Up

78% of jobs go to the first company to respond. Automated estimate follow-up sequences close 20-35% more jobs with zero extra effort.

04 -- Maintenance Contract Attrition

Most companies lose 30-40% of SMA contracts yearly because renewals are tracked on spreadsheets or simply forgotten.

05 -- Review Gap

HVAC is hyper-local and SEO-driven. Most owners do great work but never ask. Automated post-job review requests add 20-50 reviews/month.

06 -- Invoice Delays

Average HVAC company carries $15-40k in outstanding receivables at any time -- mostly due to process, not customer refusal.

07 -- Dead Lead Reactivation

Most companies have 500-2,000+ past customers never re-contacted. One reactivation campaign typically generates $10-30k in new jobs within 30 days.

08 -- Dispatch Chaos

Without smart scheduling, techs waste 45-90 min/day in dead drive time. At $75/hr loaded cost, that's $1,500-$3,000/tech/month in pure waste.

Industry Language

Terms to Know

Service call -- diagnostic visit, $75-150
SMA / Maintenance agreement -- recurring annual contract, $150-400/yr
Install -- full system replacement, $4,000-$15,000
Truck roll -- dispatching a tech, costs ~$75-150 loaded
Callback -- return visit for unsatisfied repair
Flat rate vs T&M -- flat rate pricing vs time & materials
